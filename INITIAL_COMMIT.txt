Initial commit: EchoTimeline - Family photo timeline app

Features:
- React 18 + Vite + Tailwind
- Firebase Auth/Storage/Firestore
- Drag-drop bulk photo uploads
- EXIF date extraction + AI auto-sort
- Interactive map with Leaflet
- Voice captions via MediaRecorder
- 4K video export (FFmpeg.wasm)
- PDF yearbook export (jsPDF)
- Responsive: Mobile/Tablet/Desktop
- Dark mode support
- PWA ready
- SEO optimized landing page
